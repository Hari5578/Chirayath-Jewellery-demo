    About Us

    Welcome to Chirayath Jewellery, where Jewellery crafted with Love, and every piece tells a story of heritage and elegance. Located in the vibrant heart of Chalakudy, our family-owned jewellery has been an integral part of the local community for several decades. Starting from modest origins, we've bloomed into a renowned symbol of excellence and reliability within the empire of exquisite jewelry. Our story is deeply rooted in Indian culture and craftsmanship. Inspired by our heritage, we blend traditional and modern styles to create timeless jewelry pieces.Each design reflects our dedication to preserving our cultural legacy.But beyond the allure of our jewelry, what truly sets us apart is our dedication to customer satisfaction.We strive to create meaningful relationships with each of our clients, offering personalized serviceand expert guidance to help them find the perfect piece for every occasion.As we journey ahead, we remain dedicated to the values of integrity, craftsmanship, and exceptional service that define Chirayath Jewellery. Thank you for choosing us to be a part of your special moments, and we eagerly anticipate serving you for years to come.

    Ella

    Chirayath Jewellery has launched its exclusive diamond brand, Ella Diamonds, renowned for its graceful and top-tier diamond jewelry. With a focus on precision cutting, flawless clarity, and masterful craftsmanship, each Ella Diamonds piece radiates elegance and sophistication. Ella Diamond collection features timeless designs that showcase the brilliance and sparkle of high-quality diamonds. Each piece is carefully crafted using diamonds of exceptional clarity and brilliance, ensuring that they meet the highest standards of purity. Whether it's classic solitaire rings, elegant diamond necklaces, or stunning earrings, the Ella Diamond collection offers matchless beauty and craftsmanship for those seeking luxury and uniqueness in their jewelry..
