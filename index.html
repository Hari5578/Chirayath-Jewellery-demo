    About Us

    Welcome to Chirayath Jewellery, where each ornament is crafted with love, heritage and elegance. Located in the vibrant heart of Chalakudy, our family-owned jewellery has been an integral part of the local community for over 40 years. From humble beginnings, we've blossomed into a symbol of excellence and reliability in the realm of exquisite jewellery. Our journey is steeped in Indian culture and craftsmanship, blending traditional and modern styles to create timeless treasures.
    What truly distinguishes us is our unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction. We forge meaningful relationships with each client, offering personalized service and expert guidance to help them find the perfect piece for every occasion. As we continue on this journey, we remain steadfast in our values of integrity, craftsmanship, and exceptional service. Thank you for choosing Chirayath Jewellery to accompany you on your special moments. We eagerly look forward to serving you for years to come.

    Ella

    Chirayath Jewellery proudly introduces its exclusive diamond brand, Ella Diamonds, renowned for its exquisite and premium diamond jewelry. With a meticulous focus on precision cutting, flawless clarity, and expert craftsmanship, every piece from the Ella Diamonds collection exudes grace and sophistication. The Ella Diamond collection showcases timeless designs that highlight the brilliance and sparkle of top-tier diamonds. Each piece is meticulously crafted using diamonds of exceptional clarity and brilliance, ensuring they adhere to the highest standards of purity and quality.From classic solitaire rings to elegant diamond necklaces and stunning earrings, the Ella Diamond collection offers unparalleled beauty and craftsmanship for those who seek luxury and distinction in their jewelry.
